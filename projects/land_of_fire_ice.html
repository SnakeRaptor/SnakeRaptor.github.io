Projects - Land of Fire and Ice

This page is all about one of my current modding projects for Hearts of Iron IV.

What is "Land of Fire and Ice"?

"Land of Fire and Ice" is a mod I'm creating for the Paradox Interactive game Hearts of Iron IV. The aim is to give Iceland a part to play in the game. It isn't anywhere near completion, but I'll keep this page updated as time goes on.

Why am I creating this mod?

I recently took a trip to Iceland, and the country is absolutely incredible. From mountains to mud pools, glaciers to geisers, the country really has a bit of everything. I decided it deserved a place as a strong nation in HoI4, even if it was a little historically innacurate...
